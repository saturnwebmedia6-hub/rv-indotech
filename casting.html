Welcome to RV Indotech Casting Services

At RV Indotech, we are your trusted partner for all your casting needs. With a commitment to excellence and precision, we provide a comprehensive range of casting services that cater to diverse industries and applications. Our state-of-the-art facilities, skilled artisans, and cutting-edge technologies ensure that your casting requirements are met with the highest quality standards.

Our Casting Services

Investment Casting:Our investment casting process combines intricate design capabilities with precision manufacturing. This method is ideal for creating complex and detailed components with excellent surface finishes.

Sand Casting:With our sand casting expertise, we can produce larger and more robust parts, making it a versatile option for various industrial applications.

Die Casting: Our die-casting services are known for their speed and efficiency in producing high-volume parts with consistent quality. This method is particularly suitable for lightweight and intricate designs.

Lost Wax Casting: This ancient technique, perfected by modern technology, allows us to create finely detailed and dimensionally accurate components that meet even the most demanding specifications.

Gravity Die Casting:Offering a balance between cost-effectiveness and quality, gravity die casting is a reliable choice for producing medium to large-sized components.

Industries We Serve

Automotive:From engine components to intricate interior parts, we contribute to the innovation and reliability of the automotive industry.

Aerospace:Our precision casting supports the aerospace sector, ensuring the safety and performance of critical aircraft parts.

Industrial Machinery: We provide durable and high-performance castings for heavy machinery, enhancing operational efficiency and longevity.

Energy:Our casting solutions play a vital role in the energy sector, powering advancements in renewable energy and traditional power generation.

Consumer Goods:Whether it’s artistic ornaments or functional household items, our castings add value and aesthetics to everyday products.
